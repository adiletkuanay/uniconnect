Accessibility Statement

Last updated: {{ current_date|date:"F d, Y" }}

1. Our Commitment

UniConnect is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

2. Standards Compliance

We aim to meet the following accessibility standards:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)

3. Accessibility Features

Our platform includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Alternative text for images
  • High contrast mode
  • Resizable text
  • Clear and consistent navigation
  • Descriptive link text

4. Assistive Technologies

Our platform is compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnifiers
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

5. Known Limitations

While we strive to ensure accessibility, there may be some limitations:

  • Some third-party content may not be fully accessible
  • Older PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Some complex data visualizations may be challenging to access

7. Technical Information

Our platform relies on the following technologies for accessibility:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

8. Assessment Approach

We assess the accessibility of our platform through:

  • Automated testing tools
  • Manual testing by accessibility experts
  • User testing with people with disabilities
  • Regular accessibility audits

9. Updates to This Statement

We will review and update this accessibility statement regularly. We welcome your feedback on how to improve accessibility on our platform.
