General Questions

UniConnect is a platform that connects international students with universities worldwide, providing a streamlined application process and comprehensive support throughout the journey.

Click the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner, fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to start your journey!

Application Process

Browse programs, select your desired program, and click "Apply Now". Follow the step-by-step process to complete your application.

Required documents vary by program but typically include academic transcripts, language test scores, personal statement, and recommendation letters.

Program Selection

Use our program search filters to find programs matching your interests, academic background, and career goals. You can also compare programs side by side.

Yes, you can apply to multiple programs. Each application will be processed separately, and you'll need to submit the required documents for each program.

Financial Matters

Application fees vary by program and university. You'll see the exact fee when you start your application. Some programs may waive fees for early applications.

Many programs offer scholarships. You can filter programs by scholarship availability and check the specific scholarship requirements for each program.
